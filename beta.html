What DocLib Does

Private By Default

Store photos, videos, files, audio, and notes on-device with passcode, Face ID, and encrypted backups.

Import From Anywhere

Bring content in from Photos, Files, Camera, document scan, links, Private Browser, Share Sheet, and local Wi-Fi Transfer.

Built-In Multi-Play

Watch multiple videos at once with shared play, pause, mute, speed, filter, orientation, and layout controls.

Organize Fast

Use folders, subfolders, tags, favorites, smart folders, search, and built-in viewers for documents, Markdown, PDFs, and code files.
